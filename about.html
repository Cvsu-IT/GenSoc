Welcome to Our Digital Art Gallery

Showcasing the powerful and thought-provoking creations of BSIT-2D Batch 2024-2025. This collection explores the multifaceted issue of Gender-Based Violence (GBV) through the lens of digital art.

Our Mission

Inspire action: Encourage viewers to become active participants in the fight against GBV.

Raise awareness: Shed light on the critical issue of GBV in all its forms.

Promote understanding: Foster empathy and understanding of the diverse experiences of those affected by GBV.

Celebrate resilience: Showcase the strength and resilience of survivors and advocates.

The Collection

The artworks in this gallery are categorized into three key themes:

  • Empowerment: Celebrating the strength, resilience, and agency of women and marginalized genders.
  • Equality: Advocating for a world where all individuals, regardless of gender, have equal rights and opportunities.
  • Violence: Depicting the realities of GBV, including its impact on individuals, families, and communities.
